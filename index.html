Cube Tac Toe
X Player X — place your mark

How to play

Two players share the cube. X goes first, then O.
  • Drag empty space to rotate & inspect the cube.
  • On your turn, click an empty white sticker to place your mark.
  • Then you must make one cube move with the buttons below.
  • Get 3 in a row on any single face to win.
  • A win only counts after your move finishes — and only your own line wins on your turn.
